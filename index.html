Everything you need to scale

Powerful tools wrapped in a minimalist, distraction-free interface.

Fast POS Checkout

Search items, adjust quantities, and process payments with a few clicks. The POS is stock-aware - out-of-stock items are blocked at the point of sale, preventing overselling before it happens.

Smart Stock Alerts

Cashiers are instantly warned when an item's stock drops to 5 units or fewer. Items that reach zero are automatically locked out of the POS - no override, no exceptions.

Instant Receipts

Every transaction generates a detailed receipt with a unique ID, itemized breakdown, discount applied, payment type, and cashier name.

Smart Discount System

Apply flat-amount or percentage discounts at checkout. Cash change is calculated automatically and printed on the receipt.

Multi-Store Support

Got multiple branches? Assign staff to specific locations and view consolidated financial reports across all your stores.

Excel Export & Statements

Filter your transactions by day, week, or month, and download structured Excel spreadsheets for your accounting.

Audit Logs

Premium users get a full tamper-evident action log: every item change, staff adjustment, and transaction is timestamped and attributed.

Subscription Grace Periods

We know billing hiccups happen. Enjoy a 2-day grace period on overdue subscriptions so your storefront never stops operating.

Multi-Currency Support

Set your local currency at registration - ZAR, BWP, LSL, or NAD. All prices, receipts, and reports display in your currency. Subscriptions are billed in ZAR; international customers are charged at the prevailing exchange rate.
